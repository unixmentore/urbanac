Microfiber Technology

URBANAC has replaced conventional cleaning cloths with an anti-microbial impregnated micro-fiber system, which cleans greener and dryer while removing more dust contaminants. URBANAC uses a four-color coded microfiber system, which virtually eliminates cross-contamination. A highlight of this approach is that the orange microfiber cloths are only used for disinfecting restrooms, which reduces the spread of germs and harmful bacteria to other areas of your property. Our microfiber mops also work efficiently to clean and disinfect floor surfaces with fewer chemicals and faster results
